Our Services

Our team of Travel Agents and Consultants are all fully trained in providing the following services:

Flight Reservations

As an IATA accredited travel agency MetroBase offers cost effective flight reservations to all destinations. Preference for any particular airline is of course taken into consideration whenever reservations are made. Trip Travel’s association with Tourvest Travel Services gives Trip Travel a firm representation in the international arena.

Accomodation

MetroBase takes pride in giving the customers a choice of their specific accommodation needs, no matter where – regionally, nationally, and internationally. Certain corporate discounts are available, with preferred accommodation establishments (with bill back facilities).

Car Hire

MetroBase is an agent for a number of internationally recognized car rental companies. A mutually beneficial relationship exists with Europcar, Budget and Avis from with special rates which are passed onto our customers. However, should the customer prefer any other car rental company, Trip Travel will oblige.

Leisure Travel

Trip Travel offers unforgettable team building experiences that include a wide range of activities such as 4×4 trips undertaken between Luderitz and Walvis Bay.

Visas

Trip Travel has a department which offers professional and timeous visa services. The department also advises clients regarding visa requirements and/or any other information relevant to a specific destination.

Accounting Services

Trip Travel issues an invoice to the customer on receipt of a customer’s instruction and post the following day. Car hire, tours, hotel, etc., are billed on receipt of the supplier’s invoice. Statements reflecting full travel particulars are posted on the 2nd day of each month. Statements are payable within seven (7) days from the date of the issued statement. A customer control account can be generated whenever requested
